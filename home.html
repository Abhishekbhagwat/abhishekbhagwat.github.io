Hi, I'm Janhavi.

Hi, I'm Janhavi. I'm a freshman at Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts, currently pursuing Interior & Exhibition design. I'm passionate about redefining the way design is viewed. The objective of redefining is to put forward the ideology and story behind the design rather than just the aesthetic. As a result of my exposure to an array of themes related to psychology, my art is largely introspective and reflexive with every piece. I hope to create more art that brings about constructive change in society. I take an effort to engage with diversified and unconventional subjects-ranging from nature to delineate real life scenes. I have always been intrigued by different art styles and mediums. My works make use of a varied palette of hues and textures.
